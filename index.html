Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about purchasing, launching, and managing your new business blueprint.

What do I actually receive when I buy a blueprint?

You receive the complete production files (index.html, style.css, script.js), custom documentation guide, and any styling assets. If you choose our Support Plan, we configure your domain, setup caching, hook up Formspree, and launch it live for you.

Do I need complex software or hosting to run these websites?

Not at all! Because they are coded in standard HTML/CSS/JS without heavy database backends, they can be hosted for free or next-to-nothing on platforms like Netlify, Vercel, or GitHub Pages. No complex WordPress installations or updates required.

Can I change the colors, images, and texts myself?

Yes, absolutely. The code is carefully commented and comes with a 10-page Customisation Guide. Any plain text editor can edit the index.html. We design the layouts specifically so you can change content yourself without paying developer hours.

How do the contact and booking forms send emails?

By default, forms handle validation client-side and show a success screen. To send real emails, we connect the form endpoint to Formspree, a reliable and free-tier service that forwards submissions instantly to your email inbox.
